# three.pictures DESIGN.md

## Product
three.pictures is a browser-based photo booth that always captures exactly three photos and turns them into a collectible strip.

## Core product rules
- Always exactly 3 photos.
- Privacy-first feeling.
- Camera first, no clutter.
- Download is primary.
- Print is secondary and only appears after consent.
- The app should feel like a physical booth, not a software dashboard.

## Tone
Human-made, tactile, intimate, cinematic, nostalgic, editorial, understated, timeless.

## Avoid
- Generic AI SaaS aesthetics
- Purple/blue gradients
- Glassmorphism
- Glowing blobs
- Startup landing page layouts
- Symmetrical feature-card grids
- Floating decorative shapes
- Overly rounded generic cards
- Futuristic cyberpunk styling
- Cartoonish playful UI

## Visual direction
- Warm cream and paper-white surfaces
- Charcoal ink text
- Deep booth red as the main accent
- Film-black for framing
- Subtle paper texture and grain
- Realistic, restrained shadows
- Slight asymmetry where it improves personality
- Left-aligned by default

## Typography
- One elegant editorial serif for brand moments, countdowns, and special headings
- One quiet sans or mono for controls, labels, metadata, and interface text
- Typography should feel intentional and designed, not default

## Materials
- Analog photo booth
- Contact sheet / film strip references
- Print ephemera
- Small gallery or art-book website feel
- Buttons should feel tactile and physical

## UI priorities
- Strong single primary action
- Mobile-friendly spacing
- Clear camera viewfinder
- Film strip should feel printable and collectible
- Consent modal should feel calm and trustworthy

## Required states
- Idle booth
- Live camera
- Countdown
- Flash capture
- Completed strip
- Print consent modal
